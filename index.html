MFS Pre-Grader Manual Guide

1. Setup Your Vault Portfolio Account:
Enter an email address and a custom password at the top bar framework then click Register to deploy your private data ledger.

2. Front Face Auto-Fills Scanning Channels:
Upload a crisp front image file against a solid black backdrop. The integrated Smart Vision background parser reads alignment symbols and fills target catalog input details completely hands-free.

3. Reverse Alignment Balancing Rules:
Use the back tab view space to line up reverse layout metrics. Calculations are strictly weighted at 60% front facial accuracy balance and 40% reverse design balance parameters.

Swap views to open your custom virtual sheet binder album page layout grids. Cards that capture a Gem Mint or Pristine 10 standard receive an animated gold glow leaf pocket frame edge border style automatically!
