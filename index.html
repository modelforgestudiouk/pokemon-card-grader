Pre-Grader User Guide

Welcome to Model Forge Studio! Follow this standard workflow to pre-grade and archive your collection items:

Step 1: Setup Your Vault
Enter your Email and a Password and hit Register. This creates your personal digital binder ledger where your calculations will be saved securely.

Step 2: Upload Photo
Use a well-lit photo against a solid black background for maximum accuracy. Once uploaded, wait 3-4 seconds for the built-in Smart Vision to attempt an auto-fill of the card details.

Step 3: Align Borders
Drag the four RED lines to the absolute outside edges of the physical card.
Drag the four GREEN lines to the inside border where the card artwork begins.

Step 4: Mark Defects & Calculate
Inspect the card's surface. Mark the checkboxes for any whitening, scuffs, or dents. Hit Calculate to run the multi-company centering math.

Step 5: Lock into Binder
Review your results. If you are satisfied with the scan, click the blue Lock into My Binder Ledger button. This saves the calculation, names, and original image capture permanently to your profile.
