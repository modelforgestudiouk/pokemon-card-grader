MFS Pre-Grader Vault Guide

1. Vault Profiles: Register to securely sync your card ledger to the cloud.

2. Lab Scanner: Upload photos. Use the High-Pass Mapping drop-down to filter damage, and tap the photo to drop red wear markers.

3. 3D Straightening: Use the Lean/Rotate controls in the dock to digitally flatten warped card angles before calculating splits.

4. Pricing Engine: Search a card name to pull live TCG market data.

5. Custom Binder: Save cards directly to your digital portfolio. Set custom album names and cover colors via the Customise Menu, and click Save Album Settings to remember them.
