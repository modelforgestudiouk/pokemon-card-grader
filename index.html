How to Align & Pregrade
  1. Upload an upright, flat picture of your trading card.
  2. Drag the 4 RED lines to line up perfectly with the absolute outside edges of the physical card.
  3. Drag the 4 GREEN lines to line up with the inside border edge of the yellow artwork frame.
  4. Tap Calculate Accurate Ratios to see your multi-company alignment grading score predictions instantly!
